Detailed Description

Represents the Mature state of a plant.

Age range: 30-119 days Health range: 30-100 Water range: 30-100 Sun exposure range: 40-100 Baseline water usage: 8 units/day Health gain when conditions met: +1 unit/day

void Mature::grow ( LivingPlant *  plant)
virtual

Handles daily growth in Mature state.

Process:

  1. Increment age by 1 day
  2. Apply water usage (8 units * season multiplier)
  3. If water ≥ 30 and sun ≥ 40, increase health by 1
  4. Check transition to Dead if:
    • age ≥ 120 OR health ≤ 0
    • On transition: health = 0, water = 0, sun = 0
